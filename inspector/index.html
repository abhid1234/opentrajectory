Open Agent-Trajectory Format · v0.1

Nobody reads their agent's traces — so they can't tell why it failed.

OpenTrajectory captures a live agent run — Claude Code, Codex, or Antigravity — into one open .ot.json, then diagnoses the failure: was the harness broken, did the model just get it wrong, or did it game the reward? One format, every agent, the reason — not just the score.
